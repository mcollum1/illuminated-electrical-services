How we work

Five things you can
count on us for.

Most of our work comes from neighbors recommending us to neighbors — which only works if we earn it on every single job. These are the promises we keep, no matter the size of the project.

01 · ON TIME

We arrive at 8 a.m.

Not a window — a time. We show up at 8 a.m. If anything ever changes — weather, traffic, a job running long — you'll hear from us before it happens, not after.

02 · RESPECT

We treat your home like our own.

Drop cloths under every workspace. We vacuum and clean up before we leave — your home looks better when we go than when we showed up.

03 · RIGHT THE FIRST TIME

No shortcuts.

We'd rather slow down and do it correctly than come back next month to fix it. Code-current wiring, neat boxes, labeled panels — the kind of work the next electrician compliments.

04 · NO SURPRISES

You sign off before we drill.

Every choice gets walked through with you first — fixture placement, switch height, can spacing, line-item cost. Nothing goes in the wall and nothing goes on the invoice that you haven't already approved.

05 · KURT ON-SITE

The owner leads every job.

Kurt leads every project personally — setting up the job and walking through the scope. His crew of two works with him full-time; they know his standards because they share them. You'll always know who's in your home and why.

Our Guarantee

If something we installed ever gives you a problem, call us. We'll come back and make it right — no questions, no charge. We stand behind our work because we did it right the first time, but if anything ever falls short of that, we want to know about it.

The honest pitch Do the work properly the first time, and the next time you call us is because you've got a new project — not because we left one half-finished.
